Common Questions

The answers prospects ask for first.

Drawn straight from the conversations we have most often. For anything not here, ask the concierge bottom-right or send us a message.

How is this different from a custom GPT?
Custom GPTs still guess and have strict data limits. RAG doesn't guess. It acts like a search engine for your private files, reads the exact text in real time, and summarizes it perfectly. When your data changes, the AI knows instantly, no retraining required.
Is our data safe with you?
Yes. We don't use your data to train public AI models. We build a secure, private bridge so the AI can look at your data inside your own walls, answer the question, and leave. Your data stays entirely yours.
Can it handle messy PDFs or unstructured data?
Yes. That's exactly what it excels at. Our system ingests unstructured text, breaks it down, and indexes it so the AI can navigate thousands of pages of messy documentation in milliseconds.
Do we have to replace our existing software?
Not at all. Our philosophy is the opposite. We don't ask you to learn a new platform. We build the AI to live inside the tools your team already uses every day, making your existing stack significantly smarter.
What systems can you connect AI to?
Pretty much anything. Standard cloud databases, custom internal APIs, older local file systems. We build the custom MCP connectors to bridge them all together.
Does the agent run with no human oversight?
Only as much as you want it to. We build these systems with human-in-the-loop guardrails. The agent can do the heavy lifting and then pause for a manager's approval before anything goes live, or operate fully autonomously once you've earned confidence.
